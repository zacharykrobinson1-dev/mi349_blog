About Me

Hey there! I’m Zachary Robinson, a computer science student at Michigan State University. I started this blog for a class project, but I’m really into how AI is changing stuff like school, gaming, and even healthcare. I love digging into tech and sharing what I learn in a way that makes sense to everyone.

I got hooked on coding in high school when I made a simple game for a science fair. Now, I’m learning about AI and machine learning, and it’s wild to see how it’s everywhere—like in my Spotify playlists or my phone’s autocorrect. I’m taking classes on web development and data science, so this blog is my way of practicing what I’m learning.

Some things I’m into:

  • Playing video games (big fan of strategy games)
  • Learning new coding tricks
  • Watching sci-fi movies about AI
  • Grabbing coffee with friends on campus

When I’m not studying or coding, you’ll probably find me gaming or hiking around East Lansing. Drop me a message on the contact page if you want to chat about AI or just say hi!
